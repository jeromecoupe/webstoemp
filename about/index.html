Meet the cook

I am a designer and front-end developer from Brussels, Belgium. My time is split between freelancing and teaching front-end development.

I am primarily a front-end developer with an interest in UX. I have been writing HTML, CSS, JavaScript and designing web experiences for the last 15 years and quite enjoy staying on top of it all.

I teach front-end technologies and web project management at the Institut des Arts de Diffusion (IAD) in Louvain-La-Neuve. Outside of school, I sometimes conduct workshops on CMS solutions, responsive web design or prototyping in the browser.

When I am not busy designing and coding up a website or teaching, chances are I am drinking a nice Belgian beer with friends somewhere in Brussels.

Colophon

Website designed in Sketch, coded in Sublime Text on a Mac and powered by Jekyll. The build process is automated thanks to Gulp and Git makes deployments worry-free.

Webfonts are from Typekit and include JAF Bernino Sans and FF Meta Serif Web Pro. The picture for this about page was shot by the one and only Yves Druart.
